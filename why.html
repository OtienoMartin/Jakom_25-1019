Advantages

The motor has multple advantages; however we'll state three that are very fundamental.

1. It delivers the performance of a PM magnet and does not use Permanent Magnets (PM). PMs are made of rare earth minerals found in China thus eliminates the interference in the supply chain.

2. Near frinctionless operations for it is brushless. It's a DC motor that does not have carbon brushes and uses NO PM at the same time different from SRM.
3. Direct and easy speed control. Do complex circuitry for speed manipulation.

The Name and its Origin

Jakom 25-1019

In honour of my leader, hero and The People’s President Rt. Hon. Raila Amolo Odinga who was laid to rest on 19th October 2025, I chose to name this project Jakom 2025-1019. I have as well looked up to him as a Mechanical Engineer. My determination remains becoming a Professor of Mechanical Engineering (Thermofluids) and a Professor of Theoretical Physics, and I’ll dedicate these achievements to Raila and my dad who passed on to glory on 6th June 2021.

Overview

Jakom 25-1019 is not merely an improvement on existing motor technology—it's a complete reimagining of electromagnetic propulsion. I had derived an equatin that accounts for Classical and Quantum Mechanics, Gravity, Electromagnetism, Weak Nuclear Force and Strong Nuclear Force, and Jakom 25-1019 is the forefront application off this equatin. Jakom 25-1019 achieves what was previously thought impossible: near-perfect energy conversion with minimal thermal losses.

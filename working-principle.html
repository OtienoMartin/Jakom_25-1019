Core Principle

Jakom 25-1019 works on the foundational principle that a current carying conductor inside a magnetic field will always experience a force. What sets it apart from the rest of existing motors is its physical design featuring 3 separate distinct cores to allow for DC power supply, non-permanent magnet use and brushless functionality.

I developed my version of the Theory of Everything (the Unified Theory of Physics), and built this motor as the first application of the theory. It should double as a proof of the theory.

Under the the Fundamental Force Electromagnetism, I had an equation;

V2 = ΦFuR
Where; V = voltage
               Φ= constant
               F = force
               u = velocity
               R = resistance
